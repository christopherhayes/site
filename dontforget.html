Here is a short poem (or song, I guess) that I wrote so that people do not mess up my email address, christopher.k.hayes@uconn.edu

Don't Forget the K

Don't forget the K
In the middle of my name
If you send an email to
Christopher Hayes
You'll wait many days
And then finally say
Did you get it, Mr. Hayes?

Then I'll just say,
You forgot the K
You forgot the K
Okay?

I had a student once
Who emailed me every month
Who'd apparently say
Help me, Mr. Hayes
Help me, Mr. Hayes
Sadly I must say
I didn't help a single day!

If you send email my way
Don't forget the K
Don't forget the K
Okay?

My email is not
Christopher dot
Then my last name Hayes
In between there's a K
Christopher dot
Then a K and a dot
And finally Hayes!

When you type in my name
Don't forget the K
Don't forget the K
Okay?
